About Us

A group of education and social enthusiasts initiated a learning center in Amagachi village, Bihar, addressing the region's educational and developmental gaps. Led by Vijay Paswan, the center, established in October 2022 with limited resources, currently supports 50-60 kids without formal school access. The team comprises volunteers juggling full-time jobs to sustain both their families and this meaningful initiative.
